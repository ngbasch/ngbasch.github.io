About me

I am a Data Scientist working at the intersection of economics, statitistics, and sustainability. I tend to thrive in cross-functional roles with complex and ambiguous business problems. My career started in economic consulting, where I developed statistical models and economic testimony for litigation. More recently at Indigo Ag, I have developed machine learning models to quantify field-scale agricultural emissions using satellite imagery. I enjoy working with non-data experts to translate their (often-overlooked) subject matter expertise into useful tools and models.
